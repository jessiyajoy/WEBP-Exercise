    Description

    In this assigment, you will build the classical hungry snake game in JAVA. For those of you who are not familiar, in hungry snake game a long serpent slithers across a limited field, obeying the player's orders (moving right, left, down, and up). with no way to stop it from moving. And the snake should not touch the boundaries and shouldn't bite itself or you'll die! Along the way, the creature encounters food that makes it even longer.
